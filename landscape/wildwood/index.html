Wildwood

August 2021 - ongoing

Overview

The Wildwood home contained an old historic stagecoach waiting area that was slowly falling down hill and aging into disrepair. The backyard was mostly a mud pit with awful drainage and nearly no upkeep.

Project goals

  • Repair & rehab folly
  • Build deck off folly to increase capacity & usability
  • Create tiered walkways and standing areas to maximize usable area on slope
  • Firepit area
  • Minimize mud and add better backyard access
  • Rail in driveway to create additional gathering space
